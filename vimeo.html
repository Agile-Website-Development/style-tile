This could be useful in a variety of contexts, for instance, you could hook into google analytics to see how many times a video was played.

For now though, the quote box simply disappears when you start to play the video re-appear whenever you pause the video or the video finishes.

The quote is just made up of HTML and CSS with the class name of "video-quote"
